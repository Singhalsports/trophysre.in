About us

We, Singhal Sports, established in the year 2012, are considered to be among the prominent manufacturers, suppliers, distributors, wholesalers, traders and retailers of Trophies & Mementos. We offer a wide range of products such as Acrylic Awards, Promotional Products and Signage & Display Systems. Their impeccable finish, lustrous appearance, trendy design and durable construction, make these Trophies & Mementos highly demanded. School, colleges and corporate offices are some of places where the offered trophies & mementos are highly demanded. The offered trophies & mementos can be customized at our end based on their sizes, designs, patterns, colors and materials, provided by the patrons. Moreover, the offered trophies & mementos are available with us at cost effective prices. With the aid of our well-equipped infrastructure and skilled professionals, we have been able to meet the precise needs of our patrons in the most efficient manner. Some of the machines installed at our infrastructure are embossing machines and wood cutters. Quality of the offered Trophies & Mementos is never compromised at our end, by following quality assurance procedures. With the aid our large production capacity, we have been able to meet the bulk demands in the most efficient manner.
